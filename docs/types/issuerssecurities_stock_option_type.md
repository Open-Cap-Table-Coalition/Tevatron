### Issuerssecurities Stock Option Type

**Description:** _Enumeration of stock option grant types.

 - ISO: The stock options are incentive stock options (ISOs).
 - NSO: The stock options are non-qualified stock options (NSOs).
 - OTHER: The stock options are neither ISOs nor NSOs._

**Type:** `STRING`

**Allowed Values:**
- `ISO`
- `NSO`
- `OTHER`
