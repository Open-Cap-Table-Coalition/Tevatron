### Convertible Cancellation Reason

**Description:** _The reason the convertible note was canceled.

 - CONVERTIBLE_CANCELLATION_REASON_CANCELED: The convertible note was explicitly canceled.
 - CONVERTIBLE_CANCELLATION_REASON_CONVERTED: The convertible note was converted into equity._
