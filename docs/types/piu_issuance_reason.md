### Piu Issuance Reason

**Description:** _The reason a PIU was created. Indicates what originated the PIU.

 - PIU_ISSUANCE_REASON_ISSUED_FROM_SHARE_RESERVE: PIU was issued directly from the share reserve.
 - PIU_ISSUANCE_REASON_SHARE_CLASS_CONVERTED: PIU was created as a result of a share class conversion.
 - PIU_ISSUANCE_REASON_TRANSFERRED: PIU was created as a result of a transfer._

**Referenced By (1):**
- [Piu Issuance Transaction](piu_issuance_transaction.md)

**Type:** `STRING`

**Allowed Values:**
- `PIU_ISSUANCE_REASON_ISSUED_FROM_SHARE_RESERVE`
- `PIU_ISSUANCE_REASON_SHARE_CLASS_CONVERTED`
- `PIU_ISSUANCE_REASON_TRANSFERRED`
