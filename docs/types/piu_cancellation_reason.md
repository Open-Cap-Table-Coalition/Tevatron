### Piu Cancellation Reason

**Description:** _The reason the PIU was canceled or terminated.

 - PIU_CANCELLATION_REASON_CANCELED: The PIU was explicitly canceled.
 - PIU_CANCELLATION_REASON_TERMINATED: The PIU was terminated due to the holder's relationship ending.
 - PIU_CANCELLATION_REASON_TERMINATION_FORFEITED: Unvested units were forfeited following termination.
 - PIU_CANCELLATION_REASON_REPURCHASED: The PIU shares were repurchased by the issuer.
 - PIU_CANCELLATION_REASON_TRANSFERRED: The PIU was transferred to another holder.
 - PIU_CANCELLATION_REASON_SHARE_CLASS_CONVERTED: The PIU's share class was converted._
