### Rsa Cancellation Reason

**Description:** _The reason the RSA was canceled or terminated.

 - RSA_CANCELLATION_REASON_CANCELED: The RSA was explicitly canceled.
 - RSA_CANCELLATION_REASON_TERMINATED: The RSA was terminated due to the holder's relationship ending.
 - RSA_CANCELLATION_REASON_TERMINATION_FORFEITED: Unvested shares were forfeited following termination.
 - RSA_CANCELLATION_REASON_REPURCHASED: The RSA shares were repurchased by the issuer._
