### Exercise Type

**Description:** _Enumeration of exercise types.

 - CASH_EXERCISE: The cost of exercise was covered in cash.
 - CASHLESS_EXERCISE: The cost of exercise was covered by selling shares.
 - NET_EXERCISE: The cost of exercise was covered by withholding shares.
 - BLENDED: The cost of exercise was covered by a combination of cash and selling of shares._

**Type:** `STRING`

**Allowed Values:**
- `CASH_EXERCISE`
- `CASHLESS_EXERCISE`
- `NET_EXERCISE`
- `BLENDED`
