### Publicapiissuers Stakeholder Relationship

**Description:** _The relationship of the stakeholder with the draft issuer.

 - ADVISOR: Stakeholder is an advisor
 - EX_ADVISOR: Stakeholder is an ex-advisor
 - BOARD_MEMBER: Stakeholder is a board member
 - CONSULTANT: Stakeholder is a consultant
 - EX_CONSULTANT: Stakeholder is an ex-consultant
 - EMPLOYEE: Stakeholder is an employee
 - EX_EMPLOYEE: Stakeholder is an ex-employee
 - EXECUTIVE: Stakeholder is an executive
 - FOUNDER: Stakeholder is a founder
 - INTERNATIONAL_EMPLOYEE: Stakeholder is an international employee
 - INVESTOR: Stakeholder is an investor
 - OFFICER: Stakeholder is an officer
 - OTHER: Stakeholder has some other type of relationship_
