### Issuerssecurities Exercise Period

**Description:** _- EXERCISE_PERIOD_DAY: The exercise period will be expressed in days
 - EXERCISE_PERIOD_MONTH: The exercise period will be expressed in months
 - EXERCISE_PERIOD_YEAR: The exercise period will be expressed in years_

**Type:** `STRING`

**Allowed Values:**
- `EXERCISE_PERIOD_DAY`
- `EXERCISE_PERIOD_MONTH`
- `EXERCISE_PERIOD_YEAR`
