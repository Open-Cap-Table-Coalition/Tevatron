### Convertible Interest Accrual Period

**Description:** _The length of time over which the interest due to the lender is calculated.

 - CONVERTIBLE_INTEREST_ACCRUAL_PERIOD_DAILY: Interest accrues daily.
 - CONVERTIBLE_INTEREST_ACCRUAL_PERIOD_WEEKLY: Interest accrues weekly.
 - CONVERTIBLE_INTEREST_ACCRUAL_PERIOD_MONTHLY: Interest accrues monthly.
 - CONVERTIBLE_INTEREST_ACCRUAL_PERIOD_MONTHLY_FIRST_OF_MONTH: Interest accrues monthly on the first of each month.
 - CONVERTIBLE_INTEREST_ACCRUAL_PERIOD_SEMI_ANNUALLY: Interest accrues semi-annually.
 - CONVERTIBLE_INTEREST_ACCRUAL_PERIOD_ANNUALLY: Interest accrues annually.
 - CONVERTIBLE_INTEREST_ACCRUAL_PERIOD_QUARTERLY_CALENDAR: Interest accrues on a quarterly calendar basis.
 - CONVERTIBLE_INTEREST_ACCRUAL_PERIOD_SEMI_ANNUALLY_CALENDAR: Interest accrues on a semi-annual calendar basis.
 - CONVERTIBLE_INTEREST_ACCRUAL_PERIOD_ANNUALLY_CALENDAR: Interest accrues on an annual calendar basis._
