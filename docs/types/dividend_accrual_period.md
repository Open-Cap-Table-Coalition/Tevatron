### Dividend Accrual Period

**Description:** _- DAILY: Dividends accrue on a daily basis
 - WEEKLY: Dividends accrue on a weekly basis
 - MONTHLY: Dividends accrue on a monthly basis
 - QUARTERLY: Dividends accrue on a quarterly basis
 - ANNUALLY: Dividends accrue on an annual basis_

**Type:** `STRING`

**Allowed Values:**
- `DAILY`
- `WEEKLY`
- `MONTHLY`
- `QUARTERLY`
- `ANNUALLY`
