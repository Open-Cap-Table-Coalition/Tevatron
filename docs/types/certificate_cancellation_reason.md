### Certificate Cancellation Reason

**Description:** _The reason the certificate was canceled or terminated.

 - CERTIFICATE_CANCELLATION_REASON_CANCELED: The certificate was explicitly canceled.
 - CERTIFICATE_CANCELLATION_REASON_TERMINATED: The certificate was terminated due to the holder's relationship ending.
 - CERTIFICATE_CANCELLATION_REASON_TERMINATION_FORFEITED: Unvested shares were forfeited following termination.
 - CERTIFICATE_CANCELLATION_REASON_REPURCHASED: The certificate shares were repurchased by the issuer.
 - CERTIFICATE_CANCELLATION_REASON_TRANSFERRED: The certificate was transferred to another holder.
 - CERTIFICATE_CANCELLATION_REASON_SHARE_CLASS_CONVERTED: The certificate's share class was converted._
