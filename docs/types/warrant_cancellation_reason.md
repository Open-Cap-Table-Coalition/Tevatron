### Warrant Cancellation Reason

**Description:** _The reason the warrant was canceled or terminated.

 - WARRANT_CANCELLATION_REASON_CANCELED: The warrant was explicitly canceled.
 - WARRANT_CANCELLATION_REASON_LIFETIME_ENDED: The warrant expired at the end of its lifetime.
 - WARRANT_CANCELLATION_REASON_TRANSFERRED: The warrant was transferred to another holder._
